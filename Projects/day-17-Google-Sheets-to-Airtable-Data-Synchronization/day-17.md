Google Sheets to Airtable Data Synchronization

## Objective

Built a workflow in n8n to automatically transfer data from Google Sheets to Airtable.


## Workflow

Manual Trigger


Google Sheets (Read Rows)


Airtable (Create Record)


## Technologies Used

- n8n
- Google Sheets
- Airtable


## Workflow Explanation

### 1. Google Sheets

Read candidate information from a Google Sheet.


### 2. Airtable

Created new records in an Airtable base by mapping data from Google Sheets.
