# Day 1 - Google Forms → Gmail Automation

## Objective
Create an automation that sends an email whenever a Google Form receives a new response.

## Tools Used
- Zapier
- Google Forms
- Gmail

## Workflow

Trigger:
- Google Forms: New Form Response

Action:
- Gmail: Send Email

Flow:
Google Forms → Gmail

## What I Learned

- Trigger = the event that starts the automation.
- Action = the task performed after the trigger.
- Automation follows:
  WHEN something happens → DO something.

## Result

Successfully tested the workflow and received an email notification.
